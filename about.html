About

Sketchbook studies, finished pieces, and experiments in mark-making.

Hi, I’m Alex Smith — an artist working primarily in ink, charcoal, and mixed media. I’m interested in light, texture, and the small details you notice when you slow down and draw.

This portfolio is a selection of studies and finished work. If you’d like to collaborate, commission a piece, or ask about any work here, feel free to reach out.
